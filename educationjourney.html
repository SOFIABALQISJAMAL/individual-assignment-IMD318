My Highschool Journey


A glimpse of my study journey

I studied at SMK Bukit Gading, and my high school years were filled with fun memories and meaningful friendships. During Form 4 and Form 5, I took Accounting and Economics as my elective subjects, which were challenging at first but slowly became subjects I truly enjoyed. With the support of my teachers and friends, I managed to score an A- for both subjects in my SPM. Achieving 7As in my SPM was one of the proudest moments of my high school journey, and it is a time I will always look back on fondly.

My Diploma Life


Moments from my diploma life at UiTM Kampus Rembau

My diploma life has been one of the most meaningful chapters of my journey. Studying at UiTM Kampus Rembau has taught me not only about academics, but also about friendship, independence, and self-growth. Throughout my diploma years, I have met amazing friends who supported me through stressful weeks, late-night assignments and exam seasons .
We studied together, explored Rembau, and created memories that I will always cherish . There were moments when things felt overwhelming, but these challenges helped me become stronger, more confident, and more determined.
Every experience either good or bad has shaped who I am today.
