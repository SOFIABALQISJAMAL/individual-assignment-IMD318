I’ve always wanted to go out and travel with my friends and family, exploring many places with the people I love. The peace that New Zealand seems to offer is something I truly long for. I imagine it as a place where I could escape the noise of everyday life and clear my mind.

The journey would be filled with exciting adventures and opportunities for self-reflection .
